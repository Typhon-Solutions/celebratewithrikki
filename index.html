About

Bespoke Ceremonies

Welcome, my name is Rikki Gross and I am a Commonwealth-registered marriage celebrant.

I perform personalized marriage ceremonies in Brisbane, Sunshine Coast, Gold Coast, Byron Bay and Toowoomba regions.

In addition to weddings, I conduct a range of life ceremonies such as commitment ceremonies, renewal of vows, naming ceremonies & celebrations of life.

About me

Why did I become a Marriage Celebrant? After all the emotions of childbirth had to offer, my mind wanted to only focus on the celebration of life and what it has to offer. After some time, I decided that celebrating marriages was something incredibly unique and a truly happy time in a couple’s life. This ‘something’ was why I wanted to be a marriage celebrant! Designing and delivering ceremonies seemed to be a perfect fit.

What makes me a unique Marriage Celebrant? Include you in every aspect of creating your ideal marriage ceremony. I have no joy in creating 2 template ceremonies and reusing them over and over again. When I present at a wedding I am not a hog with a microphone bossing people around, but a person who captures my couple’s personalities and ‘goes with the flow’ of a wedding! I ensure to help you along every step of the way, and endeavour to uphold your wedding ceremony expectations.

What sums me up? I am a person who is genuinely interested in a couple’s love story, have somewhat of an organisation obsession, take great pride and responsibility in my work and am a great public speaker! Life is a special gift in itself, there is no better excuse than to bring family and friends together and create fond memories which last a lifetime.
