About

Bespoke Ceremonies

Hi, my name is Rikki Gross and I am a Commonwealth-registered marriage celebrant.

I perform personalized marriage ceremonies in Brisbane, Sunshine Coast, Gold Coast, Byron Bay and Toowoomba regions.

In addition to weddings, I conduct a range of life ceremonies such as commitment ceremonies, renewal of vows, naming ceremonies & celebrations of life.

About me

I am mother to a beautiful baby boy and wife to a fabulous husband. These roles embody my qualities of patience, commitment, passion, love, communication, determination, challenge, charisma and sometimes sarcasm.

Before marriage ceremonies, I was a Youth Development Officer for an international charity organisation. In this role I developed programs and events to foster learning and collaboration in disadvantaged kids. I nutted out what I believe is a useful skillset, comprising of public speaking, creating and organising things/events, communicating and conscientiousness. Delivering ceremonies seemed to be a perfect fit!

I take great pride in my work whether it be communicating with you, submitting legal paperwork, crossing t’s and dotting i’s. What makes me unique is that I genuinely enjoy celebrating life, bringing people together for any excuse that creates smiles and creating fond memories which last a lifetime.

Some of my passions include gardening, cooking, embroidering and learning to sew, baking treats, organising things, listening to music by Kate Miller Heidke and heading to the beach with my family.
